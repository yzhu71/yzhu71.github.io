Research

Working Paper · 2024

Labor Conflict and Technological Change: A Formal Model of Preemptive Strikes

I develop a two-stage extensive-form game to explain why unions strike preemptively before technological adoption rather than after, using U.S. maritime containerization as the empirical case. The model generates propositions about preemptive strikes, protective contract demands, and post-adoption quiescence.

Formal Theory Labor Politics Game Theory
Working Paper · 2024

Detecting Censorship Events on Chinese Weibo: A Computational Pipeline

A reproducible binary classification pipeline for identifying censorship events in Chinese social media, using fine-tuned RoBERTa with SHAP explainability analysis and BERTopic topic modeling on predicted-positive posts.

Computational Methods NLP Chinese Politics
Project · 2023

U.S. Newspaper Coverage of Artificial Intelligence: Framing and Sentiment

A computational text analysis of how major U.S. newspapers cover AI, combining BERTopic topic modeling, LLM-validated sentiment analysis, and entity-level stance extraction using dependency parsing.

Media Analysis BERTopic Sentiment Analysis
